Who We Are

Founded on Genesis 26:22, Restored Fountain Tabernacle is a ministry of restoration committed to the uncompromised Word of God.

Vision

To be a centre of restoration, demonstrating the love of Jesus Christ and His healing grace to our community, nurturing disciples for the spreading of the Gospel through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Mission

Restored Fountain Tabernacle (RFT) is a ministry of restoration committed to restoring lives spiritually, physically, and mentally through the preaching and teaching of the uncompromised Word of God. We seek to restore the spiritually lost to God through Jesus Christ, lead believers into holy and righteous living, minister to the spiritually wounded, and restore the people of God back into ministry.

Core Values

Spiritually dynamic through evangelism and revival, spiritually challenging through mentoring toward righteous living, and spiritually compassionate through counselling and restoration of the wounded.
